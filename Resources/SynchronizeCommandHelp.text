Synchronize data from MOC Files.

Run this command to update data on your local development version with live-data.

The command takes two arguments, the first is the sitename on your local machine. This is the foldername under
/home/sites/SITENAME. The seconda, is an alias of a site. See all available aliases with rosemary alias command.

Example:

From moc-files
  <info>rosemary sync sitename alias</info>
